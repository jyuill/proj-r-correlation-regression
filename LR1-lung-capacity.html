Check validity of assumptions

Assumptions 1. Y-values (or the errors) are independent + requires knowledge of the study design or data collection 2. Y-values can be expressed as a linear function of the X variable 3. Variation of observations around the regression line (residual standard errors (SE)) is constant (homoscedasticity) 4. For given value of X, Y values (or the error) are normally distributed

  • validation of #1 requires knowledge of the study design or data collected
  • others can be checked by examining the residuals or errors
